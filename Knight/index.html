Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IdleForge3D printing services

What kind of 3D printing services do you offer?

We create custom 3D printed products from keychains, figurines, cosplay props, home decor to personalized gifts. If you can imagine it, we can forge it!

How do I place a custom order?

Just DM me on Instagram or WhatsApp. You can: Send your STL/OBJ file or - Pick something from our catalog or - choose a design from MakerWorld I’ll check the design, give you the price, and once you're cool with it — the printer starts cooking.

What materials can you print with?

We work with PLA and PETG filaments. We'll help you choose the best material based on durability, finish, and use.

How long does a typical 3D print take?

Print times vary based on size and complexity. Small items may take a few hours, while larger detailed prints may take 1–3 days. We provide an estimated completion time before printing.

Can you help me with 3D design if I don’t have a model?

If you don’t have a file, no worries. I can help you find a suitable design from MakerWorld or Thingiverse and customize it to fit your needs. Fully custom models from scratch aren’t available right now.

Do you ship products nationwide?

Orders are delivered anywhere in India. Delivery charges vary by location, and Chennai orders get the quickest drop-off.
